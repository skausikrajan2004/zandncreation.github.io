SHANTHALA – Synopsis

Shanthala is a fantasy adventure that bridges an ancient legend with the modern world. On a remote, uninhabited island lives a master sculptor who has raised Shanthala, a mysterious young woman blessed with extraordinary powers. Uthiran, a medical student whose father is a renowned Siddha physician and whose grandmother possesses prophetic wisdom, arrives on the island with a research team.

As Uthiran discovers Shanthala’s unique abilities, powerful forces begin to close in—one group seeks to abduct her and exploit her gift, while another aims to plunder the island’s hidden mineral wealth. When these dangerous interests converge, the fate of the island, Shanthala, and the nation itself hangs in the balance.

Blending mythology, mystery, adventure, and emotion, Shanthala unfolds a gripping journey that reveals the sculptor’s lifelong dream and a destiny that connects the ancient past with the present day.

Production Vision

Shanthala is envisioned as a grand cinematic spectacle with an estimated production budget of ₹100 Crore. The film marks the debut of Hariharan in a powerful lead role and is planned to feature an ensemble cast including Tamannaah Bhatia, Nana Patekar, Jayaram, Samuthirakani, Vinayakan, Gautham Vasudev Menon, and several other leading stars from Indian cinema. Discussions and negotiations with the cast are set to commence shortly.

Designed as a visually breathtaking fantasy adventure, Shanthala will be brought to life using cutting-edge 3D technology, blending mythology, mystery, and action on an epic scale. The project aims to collaborate with renowned Hollywood technicians and world-class visual effects artists to create an immersive cinematic experience that transcends boundaries.

With its rich storytelling, spectacular visuals, and international production standards, Shanthala is poised to emerge as one of the most ambitious and technologically advanced films in Indian cinema, offering audiences a larger-than-life journey that connects ancient legends with the modern world.

About Z & N Creation

Z and N Creation began its journey with strategic investments in the Mining Industry laying a strong foundation for long-term growth and expansion. Building on this success, the company diversified its portfolio through investments in shares of various leading companies across multiple sectors.

Over the years, Z and N Creation has also contributed significantly to urban development by acquiring and developing prime properties in major cities including Mysuru, Bengaluru, Chennai, and key cities across Kerala. Through its real estate initiatives, the company has played an active role in supporting modern infrastructure and city expansion.

Today, Z and N Creation is focused on the media and entertainment industry. Following its successful involvement in television productions, the company is now venturing into the silver screen with the production of a grand, high-budget feature film, marking a new milestone in its creative and business journey.
